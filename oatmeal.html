🥣 Oatmeal!

You need:

  • 1 cup Quaker Oats oatmeal 🌾

  • 200ml water 💧

  • 1/2 cup of milk (optional) 🥛

Steps:

  1. In a pot over medium heat heat the water until it is boiling.

  2. Dump in the oatmeal and stir. Cook for 3 minutes.

  3. When cooked, pour oatmeal and milk (if wanted) into bowls and serve immediatly. Enjoy!
