🍳 Fried Eggs!

You need:

  • 2 medium-large eggs 🥚🥚

  • A pinch of sea salt 🧂

  • A pinch of ground black pepper 🧂

Steps:

  1. Place a medium pan over medium heat and spray with cooking oil.

  2. Crack one egg, and cook until bottom side is crisp and not watery.

  3. Flip egg and cook until other side is crisp and not watery.

  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 for the other egg, but use lower heat.

  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediatly! Enjoy!
